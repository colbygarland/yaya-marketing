About YaYa Marketing

People. Focused.

We are passionate about people. People are what build businesses, products, experiences, and stories. It is the individual person who sees our products, and gets involved that matters. Each experience builds trust in our businesses, and we are about building this trust alongside you

For us social media is about connecting individual people. It is personal, as Individuals are the ones choosing what to do and look at on Social Media. We focus on the people.

We are built up of small business owners, entrepreneurs, and strategic thinkers, and as such we understand the stresses, challenges, and needs of running a business.

We focus on valuing and developing our internal team. A great team is built upon a healthy internal culture. From this our team is better engaged with you, our clients, customers, and future partners.

We are proud to be a Canadian company, working with local Canadians for our digital creation!
